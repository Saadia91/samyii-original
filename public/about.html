Hi! Welcome to my online portfolio. I am an aspiring front end web developer from London and this website is dedicated to my body of work in web development and photography. I dabbled in web design briefly at uni but I rediscovered my passion in 2017 and began learning and developing my skills. As a result, I have spent most of my time designing and creating projects using HTML5, CSS3 and Bootstrap. I enjoy creating stunning visual designs and I'm always looking to expand my skills.
